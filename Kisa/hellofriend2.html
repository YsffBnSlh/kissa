Hello Friend#2



This story is about two friends from another world, a perfect world where there is no such thing called ego and stuff.

-Hello friend.

-It’s been a while.

-Wanna hear a story?

-I would love to.

-Here is a story about a boy, a boy that dreamed about changing the world, a boy that dreamed about equality and making things right.
A boy that did not give up, he fought until the end and he won.

-He may have reached a high level, he may be able to read our thoughts.

-He is, he reached the highest level because he knows what he's doing, he finally understood himself, he found the lost piece of his soul.

-What was the lost piece?

-It was the dream.

-The dream?

-A lot of humans don’t have dreams, don’t have goals or even a reason to live.

-Then what did they live for?
-They just lived because they were afraid of dying, humans in general are afraid of things that they don’t know, but that wasn’t the case with the boy.
Because the boy didn’t live because he was afraid of dying.

-I know because he dreamed.

-“Dream” What a beautiful gift those humans had.

-But not all of them knew how to use it.
