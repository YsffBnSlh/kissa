Chill Under The Stars



What goes into your head when it's 3am and you can't sleep?

Just imagine what would it be like to just lay down under the stars at 3am.

How beautiful would it be to make an adventure inside your thoughts.

To overthink a little bit.

And to create another universe inside your head.

A place that you can run to whenever you feel like you’re starting to get lost.

Lost in your own thoughts.

The thoughts that you created.

It’s scary isn’t it?

To run from your own creation.

To hide from something you’ve made.

I wonder what would it feel like to not exist.

Is it painful to be nothing.

To not be a part of anything.

Or is it reliving.

It’s a peaceful night isn’t it.

I hope all of them are doing okay.

I wonder if any of them is thinking about me right now.

Nah they must be sleeping.

But are they dreaming about me?

I wish I could sleep so I can dream about them.

It’s been a long time since last time I slept.

*laughs* I hope I don’t get crazy.

Or maybe it’s better to be crazy.

Maybe I’ll become more creative.

Maybe it’ll make me see what other people can’t see.

Overthinking will be more fun.

But it’ll be just like living in an illusion.

Oh man, it’s morning already.

I guess you can never have enough time.
